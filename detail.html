The Tokyo Institute of Technology (Tokyo Tech) is soliciting proposals for FY2014 joint research projects under ASPIRE League Research Grant program of Tokyo Tech.

Further information on the call can be found in the attached outline or at the following website:

http://www.ipo.titech.ac.jp/aspire/english/research_collaboration/detail_780.html

The grant must be used for the purpose of conducting a joint research project. It may be used for such items:

International travel expenses Research expenses at Tokyo Institute of Technology Operation costs for the satellite laboratory Period Type 1: 1 June 2014 – 31 March 2015 Type 2: 1 May 2014 – 31 March 2015 Submission dateline by Professors or Associate Professors of Tokyo Tech Type 1: 31 January 2014 Type 2: 28 March 2014
Detailed information regarding the grant call can be found in the attached document. Queries may be directed to Peisi at kegpeisi@ntu.edu.sg. Faculty members who have active and potential collaborators in Tokyo Institute of Technology are encouraged to apply.
